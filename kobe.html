KOBE BRYANT: FROM CHILD TO GOAT
Early Life High School NBA Career Net Worth Legacy

Early Life

Kobe Bryant was born on August 23, 1978, in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. His father, Joe “Jellybean” Bryant, was a professional basketball player. From a young age, Kobe displayed incredible talent and work ethic.

High School Stardom

Kobe attended Lower Merion High School in Pennsylvania, where he led his team to a state championship and became a national sensation.

NBA Career

Drafted #13 overall in 1996 and traded to the Los Angeles Lakers, Kobe spent 20 seasons with the team, winning 5 NBA Championships and earning 2 Finals MVPs.

Net Worth & Earnings

Kobe Bryant's estimated net worth at the time of his passing was $600 million, from NBA salary, endorsements (Nike, Sprite), and business ventures.

Legacy & GOAT Debate

Kobe Bryant is remembered for his "Mamba Mentality," scoring records, championships, and inspiring millions. Dedication, work ethic, and passion made him one of the greatest players ever.
