MICHAEL JORDAN: FROM CHILD TO GOAT
Early Life High School NBA Career Net Worth Legacy

Early Life

Michael Jordan was born on February 17, 1963, in Brooklyn, New York, and grew up in Wilmington, North Carolina. He loved basketball from a young age and was inspired by his competitive family environment.

High School Stardom

At Laney High School, Jordan excelled in basketball, becoming the star of his team. His talent started to attract attention from college scouts.

NBA Career

Drafted #3 overall in 1984 by the Chicago Bulls, Michael Jordan won 6 NBA Championships, 6 Finals MVPs, and 5 regular-season MVP awards. He became a global icon.

Net Worth & Earnings

Michael Jordan's net worth is estimated at $2.2 billion, including NBA earnings, endorsements (Nike, Gatorade, Hanes), and ownership of the Charlotte Hornets.

Legacy & GOAT Debate

Michael Jordan is widely regarded as the greatest basketball player of all time. His competitiveness, clutch performances, and influence on the game are unmatched.
