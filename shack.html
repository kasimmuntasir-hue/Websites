SHAQUILLE O'NEAL: FROM CHILD TO GOAT
Early Life High School NBA Career Net Worth Legacy

Early Life

Shaquille O'Neal was born on March 6, 1972, in Newark, New Jersey. Growing up in a challenging environment, Shaq developed his size, athleticism, and love for basketball early on.

High School Stardom

At Robert G. Cole High School in Texas, Shaq became a dominant center, gaining attention from top college programs due to his extraordinary size and skill.

NBA Career

Drafted #1 overall in 1992 by the Orlando Magic, Shaq played for multiple teams, including the Lakers and Heat. He won 4 NBA Championships, 3 Finals MVPs, and 1 regular-season MVP, becoming one of the most dominant centers in history.

Net Worth & Earnings

Shaquille O'Neal's net worth is estimated at $400 million, earned from NBA salary, endorsements (Reebok, Pepsi, Papa John's), and business ventures.

Legacy & GOAT Debate

Shaq is remembered as one of the most dominant big men in NBA history. His size, skill, and personality left a lasting impact on basketball and pop culture.
