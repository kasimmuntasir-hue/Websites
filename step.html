STEPHEN CURRY: FROM CHILD TO GOAT
Early Life College Years NBA Career Net Worth Legacy

Early Life

Stephen Curry was born on March 14, 1988, in Akron, Ohio. He is the son of former NBA player Dell Curry. Growing up around basketball, he developed strong shooting skills but was considered undersized and overlooked by major colleges.

College Breakout

Curry played at Davidson College, where he shocked the nation by leading his team to the Elite Eight in the NCAA Tournament. His deep three-point shooting made him famous across the country.

NBA Career

Drafted in 2009 by the Golden State Warriors, Curry transformed the game with his incredible three-point range. He won multiple MVP awards and led the team to several championships. His shooting changed modern basketball forever.

Net Worth & Earnings

Stephen Curry's estimated net worth is $220 million. He earns through his NBA salary, endorsements (Under Armour, Callaway, and more), and business ventures. He is one of the highest-paid athletes in the world.

Legacy & GOAT Debate

Known as the greatest shooter of all time, Curry broke three-point records and inspired a new generation of players. Many consider him one of the greatest guards in NBA history.
